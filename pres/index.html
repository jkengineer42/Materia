How It Works

From natural language
to structured recommendations

Your description is sent to a Supabase Edge Function that streams back structured material cards via Server-Sent Events (SSE) — no waiting, results appear in real time.

The AI cross-references a LCA-certified materials database and scores each candidate across composite, ecology and economic profiles.

Every metric grounded
in certified LCA data

Materia was built for the Crusoe Sustainability Track. All CO₂ figures come from certified Life Cycle Assessment datasets — not estimates.

The tree equivalent makes savings tangible: 1 tree absorbs ~25 kg CO₂/year. Every audit shows you how many trees your choices are worth.

The dashboard aggregates across all your audits so you can track cumulative environmental impact over time.
